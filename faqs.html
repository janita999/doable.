Frequently Asked Questions

What is doable.?

doable. is an AI-powered platform that helps young people create, run, and maintain their own small businesses with personalized, step-by-step guides.

How does the AI work?

Our AI uses your input (age, interests, budget, etc.) to generate a custom business guide using advanced language models.

Is my information safe?

Yes. We do not store your personal data. Your information is used only to generate your guide and is not shared or saved.

Can I save my guide?

Yes! After your guide is generated, you can download it as a PDF for future reference.
